About Aditya Physiotherapy Clinic & Day Care Services

At Aditya Physiotherapy Clinic, we specialize in rehabilitation and restoration of human function. Led by Dr. Minakshi Gupta, our team provides expert care to patients of all ages.

Qualification: BPT, MPT (Orthopaedics & Sports Rehabilitation)

Experience: 10+ Years in Physiotherapy & Rehabilitation

Mission: To deliver compassionate, personalized, and evidence-based physiotherapy care that restores health, improves mobility, and enhances quality of life.

Our clinic offers advanced physiotherapy treatments, day care services, and personalized programs for pre & post-operative rehabilitation, sports injuries, chronic pain, and neurological conditions.

  • Electro Therapy, Manual Therapy, MFR
  • Traction, Exercise Therapy, Taping
  • Dry Needling & Cupping Therapy
  • Home Care & Ambulance Services
